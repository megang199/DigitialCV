My Travel:

My favorite places I have traveled have been Amsterdam, The Netherlands, Paris, France, and my annual summer vacation to Ocean City, New Jersey.

The Netherlands

I went to The Netherlands in July of 2019 for a field hockey camp. I went without my parents and I only knew one other person. It was an amazing experience as I got to play with other people around the world. A lot of the people I played with were on their countries national team. I stayed in a tent for the 10 days that we had our camp. I was also there when the highest recorded temperatures in the country happened, it was 100 degrees for multiple days. I really want to go back when I study abroad. The Netherlands is an amazing country and I love the way of life over there.

France

I visited Paris, France when I was 10 years old. I went with my mom, my best friend and her mom. It was our 10th birthday present to get to go together. Even though I was only 10, I still remember like it was yesterday. My favorite thing was all the croissants and baguettes. Every monring when I went downstairs I could smell the bakeries, it was heavenly. They also had super fun playgrounds that were very appealing to 10 year olds.

Every year since I was born my family has gone to Ocean City, New Jersey each summer. It is my favorite place of all time to go to, it reminds me of my childhood and brings me comfort. My great aunt and uncle used to own a house which brought together so much of my family, however sadly my great uncle passed away and they had to sell the house. However, we rent a house every year and still get to see extended family. I get to stay with my aunt and cousins. It is by far my favorite week of the entire year getting to go and relax at the beach.
