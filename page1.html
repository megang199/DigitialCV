Hi, my name is Megan Gilbey I am a sophomore at Elon University in North Carolina stuyding Strategic Communications and Psychology. I am originally from Avon, Connecticut and have 2 younger brothers. Braden is 17 years old and Ciaran is 8 years old. I love to travel, cook and bake, go for walks, and play sports. I played field hockey for 7 years for both club and school.
